Lock stablecoins for **90 days** and earn a **fixed 6.9% APR**, receiving the same amount back plus yield at maturity.

HOLLAR Stable Yield Bonds have no liquidation risk for users, as the protocol covers the underlying yield and liquidation risk. Yield is fixed when the bond is created, while returns are generated through strategies available directly on Hydration — without relying on external incentives.

Bonds have a fixed maturity date and cannot be unlocked early.

Since yield is calculated over the **full 90-day term** at the moment of creation, bonds that remain unsold for some time may provide a higher effective yield to the eventual buyer.

By participating, users also support Hydration’s broader goals around HOLLAR growth, liquidity, and long-term protocol sustainability.
