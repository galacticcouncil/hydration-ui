Decentral is revolutionizing the payment process for creators, influencers, agencies, and production companies in Brazil, who often face frustrating payment delays of **30 to 90 days**. By purchasing unpaid invoices at a discount, Decentral provides **immediate liquidity** to creators. For instance, if a creator has a **100k invoice** due in a month, Decentral might buy it for around **96k**, ensuring the creator gets paid right away while collecting the full amount later from the corporate client.

This innovative service is supported by **DUX Group**, a reputable Brazilian financial entity responsible for **origination, underwriting, credit assessment, and collections**. As the Web3 division, Decentral taps into **DeFi protocols** and **on-chain investors** to secure the necessary liquidity for these transactions.
